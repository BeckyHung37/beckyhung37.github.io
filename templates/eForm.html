ITRI eForm Platform
Overview
ITRI eForm Plaform provides a digital form application entry and a flexible form creation mechanism. Our users can submit form applications, and administrators can freely create and manage forms on the platform.

ITRI eForm Plaform provide 3 roles:
General User: The user who applicate the form.
Admin: The administrators who can create the form rules on platform.
Manager: The form manager who have to review the form which applicated from general user.
